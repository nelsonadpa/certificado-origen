01

The story

A Cuban export manager needs one key document: the certificate of origin. Once paper and queues; today, fully online.

02

What it is

The official document proving Cuban origin. Issued by the Chamber of Commerce under the Rules of Origin Regulation (Joint Res. 6/2018, WTO-aligned).

03

What it is for

With it, goods pay less duty — or none — in countries with an agreement. Without it, they enter as if from anywhere.

04

The portal

Everything happens on MINCEX's Single Window for Foreign Trade.

05

Identity

A single account, verified against official records: every application is tied to a real, accountable person.

06

The company

The exporter's details come pre-filled by the system.

07

Destination & importer

Pick the country; the system lists the registered importers and fills in their details.

08

The product

The tariff subheading is chosen from an official catalog of 2,200+ codes, with its origin criterion.

09

The agreement decides

The heart of it: the system finds the applicable agreement and picks the model — ALADI, GSP or Normal.

10

Shipment & invoice

Vessel, ports, weights, FOB value and the attached invoice: all the evidence in one submission.

11

Review

Everything consolidated on one screen before sending.

12

Bilingual, with a unique number verifiable online, paperless.

13
