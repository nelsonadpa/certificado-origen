To export Cuban goods with the advantages they deserve. Yesterday, a paper procedure; today, online. Here is how.

What is it?

It proves a good is of Cuban origin

Issued by the Chamber of Commerce of the Republic of Cuba under the Rules of Origin Regulation —Joint Resolution No. 6 of 2018—, aligned with the WTO Agreement on Rules of Origin.

What is it for?
0trade agreements with preferences

With the certificate, my goods pay lower duties —or none— in the countries Cuba has agreements with. Without it, they enter as if from anywhere, and lose that advantage.

What it generates

One data point, reported only once

Every certificate feeds an official export database. The data travels verified between institutions and borders, following international standards for trade data exchange (UN/CEFACT). Less paperwork, more trust.
